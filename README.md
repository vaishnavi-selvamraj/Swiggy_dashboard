# 🍕 Swiggy Sales Analysis Dashboard
 
An interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing Swiggy's food delivery sales across cities, restaurants, food types, and time periods.
 
![image_alt](https://github.com/vaishnavi-selvamraj/Swiggy_dashboard/blob/main/Screenshot%20(69).png?raw=true)
 
---
 
## 🔍 Overview
 
This dashboard provides a comprehensive view of Swiggy's order and revenue performance. It enables business analysts and operations teams to track sales trends, compare restaurant performance, and understand geographic demand — all with dynamic slicers for city, food type, quarter, and restaurant.

## 🧩 Dashboard Features
 
### Slicers / Filters
- **City** — Filter by delivery city
- **Food Type** — Non Veg / Veg
- **Quarter** — Q1, Q2, Q3, Q4
- **Restaurant** — Filter by specific restaurant chain
### Visuals Included
 
| Visual | Description |
|---|---|
| KPI Cards | Price sum, avg order value, total orders, avg rating, rating count |
| Total Sales by Month | Line chart — Jan to Aug trend |
| Total Sales by Restaurant | Horizontal bar — top 5 chains ranked |
| Total Sales by Food Type | Donut chart — Non Veg vs Veg (50/50 split) |
| Total Sales by Day | Bar chart — daily sales pattern across the month |
| Total Sales by State | Horizontal bar — state-wise revenue comparison |

## 🚨 Key Insights
 
- **Karnataka leads** all states in total sales, significantly ahead of others
- **KFC** is the top-performing restaurant chain by total sales
- Food type split is **exactly 50% Non Veg / 50% Veg**
- Sales trend shows a **dip in February–March** before recovering through April–August
- **Daily sales** are relatively consistent across the month with slight spikes mid-month and at month-end
